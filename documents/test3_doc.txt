The international concert tour “Echoes of Freedom” by the world-renowned artist Lana Shore will begin on June 5, 2025, in Berlin, Germany. The tour includes performances in 20 cities across Europe and North America, with major stops in Paris, London, New York, and Toronto.

Each venue has been carefully selected to host over 10,000 attendees, and VIP ticket holders will get exclusive backstage access. The production team announced that logistics will involve 12 trucks carrying stage equipment and lighting, along with a crew of over 80 professionals.

Tickets go on sale starting April 10, 2025, via the official tour website and select ticketing partners.
